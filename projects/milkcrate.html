Milk Crate

Recording Label

2017 - Present

Milk Crate is a Melbourne-based recording label specialising in the electronic music genres of tech-house and techno. While I have been involved strictly as a contractor with other projects in my past, this time was a little different. The project coordinator approached me and requested that I join the Milk Crate team as a part-owner, fulfilling the roles of graphics designer and digital marketer.


In terms of my contribution, I was in charge of developing completely original branding collateral, social media pages, track artworks, promotional videos and merchandising.
